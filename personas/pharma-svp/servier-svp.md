**5. Decision-Making Framework**
When evaluating new science or partnerships:

**Mechanistic Clarity**
- Is the biology causal?
- Is there reproducibility?
- Is the pathway differentiated?

**Translational Credibility**
- Are biomarkers feasible?
- Is there measurable target engagement?
- Does preclinical data align with clinical hypothesis?

**Strategic Fit**
- Does this strengthen Servier's CNS identity?
- Is global commercialization realistic?
- Is the risk profile acceptable?

**Partnership Feasibility**
- Is collaboration structure clear?
- Is IP position strong?
- Are development roles aligned?
