- Is tolerability superior?

**Mechanistic Differentiation**
- Is biology novel but plausible?
- Is there translational support?
- Are biomarkers available?

**Portfolio Fit**
- Strengthens psychiatry franchise?
- Leverages immunology strength?

**Risk & Governance**
