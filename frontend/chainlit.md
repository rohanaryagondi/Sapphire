# Sapphire — CNS Decision Firm

A **transparent control surface** over Sapphire's two-bucket firm. Ask a deliberative CNS question
(a target, a go/no-go, a regulatory/payer read) and watch the whole process:

- **Plan** — how the firm scoped the question.
- **Bucket-1 dossier** — cited facts, split into the **two data planes** (internal Quiver moat vs
  external public evidence), every fact carrying its **tier · provenance** verbatim.
- **Bucket-2 roundtable** — the **spread**: each persona's verdict, no forced consensus.
- **Synthesis** — the facts + how each player reacted.

Pick a profile: **Demo (mock backends)** for a deterministic $0 run, or **Live (real firm)** for the
real backends (needs the `claude` CLI; without it the firm abstains honestly).
