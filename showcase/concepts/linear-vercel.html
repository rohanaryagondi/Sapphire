Synthesis & Recommendation
CONDITIONAL PROCEED ▾
The evidence base supports prioritizing DCTN6 as the primary rescue candidate for the TSC2 phenotype. The internal moat (T1, n=847, moat-real) delivers the highest rescue score (0.89) with orthogonal EMET corroboration (PMID 31980601) and Q-Models CMAP alignment (0.87). FZD7 is a credible secondary target; the Wnt mechanism is biologically plausible and the published effect size is moderate but reproducible (PMID 29415998). MTOR carries a DIVERGENCE flag — moat signal (0.71) conflicts with the inhibitor resistance literature — and active in-force IP (WO2023/012345). The roundtable spread (3× Conditional, 1× Proceed, 1× Abstain) reflects genuine uncertainty about in vivo translatability rather than a scientific dispute about the moat data.
