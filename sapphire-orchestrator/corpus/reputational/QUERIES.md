# QUERIES — the corpus answering real Reputational-Institutional checks

Five realistic checks this agent runs at runtime (dossier field **F4**), answered **from the local corpus**
(citing the claim-card(s) / note). The last two show corpus-first → **search-the-gap** (live press + social).

Framing (from the spec): **facts only — report the perception and its source**; perception ≠ scientific
merit (keep distinct); down-weight T4; never let reputation override T1–T2 evidence.

---

### Q1. "Does an amyloid-targeting Alzheimer's program inherit a reputational headwind?"
**Answer — yes, a strong one.** Three compounding hits: the congressional finding of an "atypical FDA review
process and corporate greed" behind Aduhelm [card #1]; the SEC fraud case against Cassava/simufilam [card #2];
and Nature's retraction of the foundational Lesné Aβ*56 paper [card #3]. See `notes/`.

### Q2. "Does that reputational cloud mean the amyloid science is invalid?"
**Answer — no; keep perception separate from merit.** The Lesné retraction killed one specific claim (Aβ*56),
but the amyloid hypothesis is independently supported by human genetics — familial APP/PSEN1/PSEN2 mutations
all converge on Aβ [card #4, EMET, Lanoiselée 2017]. The agent reports the headwind **and** this distinction.

### Q3. "What's the data-integrity track-record risk for small-biotech Alzheimer's cognition claims?"
**Answer — elevated post-Cassava.** The SEC charged Cassava + execs and a scientist "for manipulating clinical
trial results," >$40M paid [card #2] — heightening institutional skepticism of bold cognition claims from
small sponsors.

### Q4. "What reputational headwind attaches to FDA accelerated approval in CNS?"
**Answer — the Aduhelm legacy.** The congressional report made FDA's accelerated-approval process itself a
credibility issue [card #1] (and drove the FDORA reforms — see corpus #3 policy-legislative). A surrogate-based
CNS accelerated approval now faces extra scrutiny.

## Checks the corpus CANNOT answer → search the gap live (the design working)

- **"What's the reputational status of psychedelics / a specific sponsor (e.g., Lykos, Sarepta)?"**
  → **GAP — harvest live.** Lykos/MDMA (AdComm misconduct allegations, journal retractions) and Sarepta
  (gene-therapy deaths) are real but not carded this pass (manifest gap #1). **STAT/Reuters/Endpoints/Retraction Watch live.**
- **"What's social/blog sentiment on this program's credibility right now?"**
  → **GAP — T4, live-only.** Social commentary is never pre-ingested and never overrides T1–T2 (gap #2).
- **"Any reputational event after the retrieval window?"**
  → **Always a live call** — reputation shifts fast (gap #5).
- **"Reputational signal for non-Alzheimer's CNS classes?"**
  → **GAP.** Corpus is amyloid/AD-heavy (gap #4). **Live search.**
