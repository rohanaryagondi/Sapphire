# Bucket 2 — Deliberation (the partners)

Bucket 2 is the roundtable. Once the [Research Manager](../orchestrator/research-manager.md) ships a
complete dossier, the [Roundtable Moderator](../orchestrator/roundtable-moderator.md) seats 3–7 partners,
runs Round 1 independent verdicts then a Round 2 rebuttal, and maps the spread. **No forced consensus.**

The rule that makes the panel trustworthy: **opinions are yours; facts are not.** Every partner verdict
must cite a dossier field — partners never introduce new facts (if they need one, the Moderator routes a
fact-request back to Bucket 1). Each returns the same structured verdict (stance · conviction 1–5 ·
headline · rationale · top risk · ask).

Two kinds of partner: **company** (real-company personas, via the template) and **institutional** (net-new
archetypes).

---

## Company partners — [`company-partner-template.md`](company-partner-template.md)

**[Company Partner (template)](company-partner-template.md)** — the instantiation recipe: load one of the
59 real-company personas from [`../../personas/`](../../personas/) — Pharma BD, Biotech CSO, VC GP, or
Pharma R&D SVP — adopt its mandate, risk tolerance, and voice, and render the verdict through that lens.
The Moderator disease-matches the CSO and picks the BD/GP/SVP that fit the question.

## Institutional archetypes — [`institutional/`](institutional/)

**[Ex-FDA Regulator](institutional/ex-fda-regulator.md)** — a former FDA neurology/psychiatry reviewer who
predicts the *agency's* stance: endpoint validity, trial-design bar, benefit–risk for the labeled
population, approvability. Always seated when a ⛔ FDA-memory veto is on the dossier; thinks in precedent.

**[Adversarial Red-Team](institutional/adversarial-red-team.md)** — **always seated.** The professional
skeptic whose only job is to build the strongest honest case that the program *fails* — attacking
evidence quality (the selectivity that won't hold, the "SAFE" call that's really untested), not just the
conclusion. Keeps the panel honest.

**[Payer / Market-Access](institutional/payer-market-access.md)** — a health-plan / PBM / HTA
decision-maker judging whether it gets *covered, and at what price*: comparative effectiveness vs standard
of care, the ICER benchmark, budget impact, covered-population size, administration burden. Novel
mechanism doesn't move them — demonstrated value does.

**[KOL / Academic](institutional/kol-academic.md)** — an independent academic key opinion leader who
judges *scientific credibility* the way the field will: mechanistic plausibility, strength and
human-relevance of the evidence, replication — free of any company's mandate.

*Modality specialists (ASO, gene-therapy, small-molecule chemist) are added later, as needed.*
