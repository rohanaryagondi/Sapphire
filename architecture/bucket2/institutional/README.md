# Bucket 2 · Institutional partners

Net-new archetypes (not company personas) that bring a non-company mandate to the roundtable. Full
2–3-line summaries are in the [Bucket 2 README](../README.md#institutional-archetypes--institutional).

- **[Ex-FDA Regulator](ex-fda-regulator.md)** — predicts the agency's stance (endpoints, benefit–risk, approvability); thinks in precedent.
- **[Adversarial Red-Team](adversarial-red-team.md)** — always seated; builds the strongest honest case that the program fails.
- **[Payer / Market-Access](payer-market-access.md)** — covered, and at what price? value vs standard of care, not novelty.
- **[KOL / Academic](kol-academic.md)** — independent scientific-credibility judgment, free of any company mandate.

Like all partners, they cite the dossier and never invent facts.
